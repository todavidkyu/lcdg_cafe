About the Cafe


"When you drink from that coffee mug,
I get to share that moment with you."

“La Café de Geilanna” is a coffee shop that serves espresso, latte, cappuccino, etc.. Some coffeehouses may serve cold drinks, such as iced coffee, iced tea, and other non-caffeinated beverages.


The La Cafe de Geilanna is open in Westchase! We are excited to introduce our Coffee Shop. If you crave great coffee, you’ll love knowing that we provide a real alternative to chains with handcrafted coffee and espresso drinks, a full tea bar, and real food options.
Our Coffee shop delivers way beyond the traditional coffee shop favorites (although we think our homemade pastries are pretty good). We have traditional breakfast choices, including eggs, omelettes, pancakes, and plenty of healthy options (including gluten free, vegetarian, and vegan). We will be having weekend specials, when we go all out to provide you with new options that are delicious and different. Finally, we also serve a wide variety of salads, sandwiches and flatbreads for lunch-lovers.
